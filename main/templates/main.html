What is Bite Tracker?

Bite Tracker is a simple web application that allows you to track your daily food intake. You can input the food you eat and the application will calculate the total calories you consume. You can also set a daily calorie goal, and the application will tell you if you have reached your goal or not.

How to use Bite Tracker?

To use Bite Tracker, you need to create an account. Once you have an account, you can log in and start tracking your food intake. You can add food items to your daily log, and the application will calculate the total calories you consume. You can also set a daily calorie goal, and the application will tell you if you have reached your goal or not.
